I have 2 ThankYou® Accounts,
how can I combine them?

Combine accounts>

You can combine multiple ThankYou Member Accounts
into one ThankYou Member Account, provided each
Sponsor Account
and the ThankYou Member Account belong to the same individual.

Remember, if you combine your ThankYou Member
Accounts, any action taken concerning points from
one Sponsor Account may affect all the points in your
ThankYou Member Account.

How do I check my current point balance?

Check point balance>

See your current points summary along with other
account information by clicking on “My Account”
and either selecting “My Points Summary” from
the drop-down menu or clicking on the link from
the “Welcome back!” page.
