How do I log on to
ThankYou.com?

Access the site and follow three easy steps:

  1. Visit thankyou.com and click “Sign On” in the
    upper right corner.
  2. Use your Account Online or Citibank Online
    username and password to sign on.
  3. Link your e-mail address to stay up-to-date
    on ThankYou® promotions and updates.
